# Bank Customer Churn Analysis

## Prepared By
Lokesh Perumal

## Tools Used
- SQL
- Power BI
- Excel
- DAX

## Project Objective
Analyze customer churn behavior, identify high-risk customer segments, quantify revenue loss, and provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention.

## Dataset
- 10,000 Customer Records
- Banking Customer Churn Dataset

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1: Executive Overview
- Total Customers
- Churned Customers
- Churn Rate
- Revenue Loss
- Churn by Geography
- Churn by Gender

### Page 2: Customer Churn Risk Segmentation
- Churn by Age Group
- Churn by Product Group
- Churn by Membership Status
- Churn by Credit Score Group
- Churn by Balance Group
- Churn by Card Type

## Key Findings
- Customers aged 50+ have the highest churn rate.
- Inactive members churn significantly more than active members.
- Germany has the highest churn rate.
- Low-balance customers are at greater risk.
- Poor credit-score customers show higher churn behavior.

## Business Impact
This project helps banks identify high-risk customer segments, reduce churn, protect revenue, and improve customer retention strategies.
